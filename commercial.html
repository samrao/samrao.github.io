We started Chronic Ink 8 years ago today. We were humbled very quickly, which has also led us to become the grateful studio we are today. Some advice for the creators of tomorrow: . You make plans to start something. It doesn’t quite work out the way you think it’s going to. Your stuff is not as cool as you thought they were going to be. Money is tight. Time is running out. . That’s normal. . Happens to everyone. (Happened to us.) Take a deep breath. Analyse the results. Don’t lie to yourself. Get back to work. Find the un-cool. Repeat some of these things. Make new things. Your stuff can be pretty cool. Life. .
